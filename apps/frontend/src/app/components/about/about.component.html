About me

Hi! I'm Jason, a frontend developer who's all about clean, efficient code and making sure things just work. I've spent years working with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Angular, turning designs into reality and ensuring everything runs smoothly. I'm all for building solid, reliable interfaces that get the job done right.

I started back when Netscape Navigator was still a thing (yes, I know!), and over the years I've had the chance to work with some amazing teams at Barclays, Fidelity Investments, Vanguard, Charles Tyrwhitt, Direct Line Group, and more. I set up an ecommerce business that ran for 12 years, have integrated countless APIs, re-branded websites, upgraded legacy apps, and built new ones from the ground up.

I've loved every minute of the journey so far. When I'm not buried in code, you'll find me in the garden, on a yoga mat, or hanging out with Oscar (the cat). I've also just launched a blog (or rather Oscar has!) to share what we've learned over the years and to hopefully add a bit of humour to this very dry subject!
